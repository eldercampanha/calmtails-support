# AI Weekly Insights

Version: 2026-05-16

CalmTails can use AI to review recent logs, find patterns, and create weekly insights for your dog. To do this, selected log details and your dog's name may be sent to OpenAI. This may include behavior logs, notes, signs, events, and medication context. AI insights are optional, can be turned off, and are informational only. They are not veterinary advice.

- Selected recent log details may be sent to OpenAI.
- Your dog's name may be sent to OpenAI so insights read naturally.
- Insights are used for app functionality, not advertising or tracking.
- Insights are informational and are not veterinary, medical, training, safety, or behavioral advice.
- A dog admin can turn AI insights off anytime.
