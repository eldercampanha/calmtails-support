Score Tracker Support

Help for setting up players and tracking scores locally on your device.

What the app does

Score Tracker lets you add players, start a game, and keep score with data stored locally on your device.

Troubleshooting

  • If a player does not appear, make sure the name was added before starting the game.
  • If your data looks missing after reinstalling the app, local-only data may have been removed with the app.
  • If the app becomes unresponsive, close and reopen it, then restart your device if needed.
