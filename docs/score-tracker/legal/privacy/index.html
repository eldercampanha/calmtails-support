Score Tracker Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-12

Score Tracker stores app data locally on your device to support player setup and score tracking during games.

Information We Collect

Score Tracker does not require account creation and does not intentionally collect personal information through a backend service. Player names and scores are stored locally on your device.

How Data Is Stored

App data is saved locally on your device. If you remove the app, locally stored data may also be removed.

Third-Party Services

At this time, Score Tracker does not rely on sign-in, cloud sync, or advertising services.

Your Choices

You can clear app data by deleting the app or using any in-app reset functionality if available in future versions.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact elderbaltazar@gmail.com.
