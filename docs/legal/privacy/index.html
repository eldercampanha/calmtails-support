Calm Tails Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-28

This Privacy Policy explains how Calm Tails ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Calm Tails app and related services.

1. Account and Authentication Data

Some versions of Calm Tails may be used without creating an account. If account or sign-in features are used now or in future releases, we may process authentication-related data such as user identifiers, email address, and basic account metadata needed to provide secure access and service functionality.

2. User Content and App Data

Calm Tails is designed for dog event logging and report generation. Information you enter, such as logs, routines, notes, and generated reports, is used to provide app features you request. Depending on app configuration, this data may be stored locally on your device and/or in service infrastructure used to sync, back up, and deliver your content across sessions.

3. Analytics and Crash Reporting

We use Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to understand feature usage, app performance, and stability issues. These services may collect technical and diagnostic information such as device data, app version, interaction events, and crash traces. This helps us improve reliability and user experience.

4. How We Use Information

  • Provide and maintain core app functionality.
  • Generate, store, and present your logs and reports.
  • Monitor usage and improve product quality, performance, and safety.
  • Communicate legal or policy updates when required.

5. No Sale of Personal Information

We do not sell your personal information.

6. Data Retention and Your Choices

We retain data for as long as needed to provide the Service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. You can manage or delete certain app data from within the app, through device settings, or by uninstalling the app (which may remove locally stored data). Where applicable, you may request access, correction, or deletion of personal data by contacting us.

7. Children’s Privacy

Calm Tails is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can review and take appropriate action.

8. International Use and Future Expansion

Calm Tails is currently operated from Canada and may expand service availability internationally over time. If you access the Service from outside Canada, you understand your information may be processed in countries where we or our service providers operate, subject to applicable legal safeguards.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post updates on this page and revise the "Last updated" date at the top.

10. Contact
